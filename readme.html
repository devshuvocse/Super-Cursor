What is SuperCursor?

SuperCursor is a browser extension that replaces your standard mouse pointer with a variety of beautiful and engaging special effects. From a subtle aura to a dazzling starfall, SuperCursor adds a touch of magic to every click and movement. It's built to be lightweight and works on any webpage.

How to Use

  1. Click the SuperCursor icon in your browser's toolbar.
  2. A popup will appear. Use the dropdown menu to select a cursor style.
  3. Click the "Apply" button.
  4. Your new cursor effect is now active! Your choice will be saved for future browsing sessions.
